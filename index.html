One Day in VBZ World

A public transport visualization coded during
Open Data Day Hackathon in Zurich

  • Züri-Fäscht 2016
  • 00:00
  • About

What is it?

The idea of this project is to visualize on a map all VBZ vehicles as the move across the city during the day, with special focus on dates of special events that affect the public transportation network.

How to use?

  1. Use the slider below the map to move through the day.
  2. Click on any tram/bus to see its route.
  3. Sip your coffee while nodding happily.
